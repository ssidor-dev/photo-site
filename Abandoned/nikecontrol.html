Nike Missle Control Site N-95

This site was originally a World War II POW camp and staging area known as Camp Patrick Henry. After the war, a portion of Camp Patrick Henry was converted into a Nike Missile Control Site in 1955 where it housed the US Army's Nike Ajax, and later in 1958, the Nike Hercules missiles. In 1971, the Nike Missile Control Site N-85 was decommissioned.
